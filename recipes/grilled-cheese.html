Grilled Cheese

"Check out that gooey stack of cheese and bread. You're gonna make that mofo."

Ingredients:

  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • 2-3 Slices of Cheese
  • 1 Tbsp Salted Butter

Steps:

  1. Assemble sandwich
  2. Melt half of butter over medium heat
  3. Place sandwich in pan and cook for 2 minutes
  4. Add remaining butter to pan and flip sandwhich
  5. Cook for 2 more minutes
  6. Remove sandwhich from pan and enjoy your Grilled Cheese
